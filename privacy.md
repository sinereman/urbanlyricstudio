# Urban Lyric Studio — Privacy Policy

Last updated: march 2026

Urban Lyric Studio does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

**Data Storage**
All songs and content created within the app are stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to any server.

**No Account Required**
No account, login, or registration is required to use the app.

**No Third-Party Tracking**
The app does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

**No Microphone Access**
The app does not request or use microphone access.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Is an internet connection required?**
No. The app works fully offline.

**Is there a subscription?**
No. Urban Lyric Studio is a one-time purchase with no hidden fees.

**Where are my songs saved?**
All songs are saved locally on your device.

**I lost my songs after reinstalling. Can I recover them?**
Unfortunately local data is deleted when the app is uninstalled. We recommend exporting your songs as PDF before uninstalling.
