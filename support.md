### Frequently Asked Questions

**Is an internet connection required?**
No. The app works fully offline.

**Is there a subscription?**
No. Urban Lyric Studio is a one-time purchase with no hidden fees.

**Where are my songs saved?**
All songs are saved locally on your device.

**I lost my songs after reinstalling. Can I recover them?**
Unfortunately local data is deleted when the app is uninstalled. We recommend exporting your songs as PDF before uninstalling.
